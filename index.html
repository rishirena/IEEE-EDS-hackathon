About the Hackathon & IEEE EDS Chapter

IEEE EDS Hackathon 2026 focuses on innovation in electronics, semiconductor devices, and intelligent engineering systems.

Why Participate?

The IEEE EDS Chapter Hackathon 2026 is a flagship technical event designed to challenge students and spark innovative thinking. Participants work on real-world engineering problems across Robotics, VLSI, and IoT domains.

The multi-round structure evaluates technical knowledge, creativity, and presentation skills. From online screening to design and implementation phases, students gain hands-on experience while competing for exciting rewards.

About IEEE EDS Chapter

The IEEE Electron Devices Society (EDS) Chapter is dedicated to advancing the theory, design, and application of electron and semiconductor devices. The chapter promotes scientific and technical knowledge in the field of electron and ion devices.

Through workshops, seminars, and technical competitions, the chapter provides students with platforms to enhance their skills and showcase their technical capabilities, fostering innovation and excellence in technology.
